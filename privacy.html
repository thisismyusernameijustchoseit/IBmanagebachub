Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Schedule Planner ("the app", "we", "us"), operated by JJY Corp. and its affiliates, collects, how it is used, and your choices. This is a non-commercial tool. By creating an account or using the app, you agree to this policy.

Who this is for

This app is intended for students at participating schools. If you are under 13 (or the minimum digital-consent age in your country), please use the app only with the awareness and permission of a parent, guardian, or your school.

What we collect

Account information

  • A username you choose (this does not need to be your real name).
  • A password, which is never stored directly. It is protected using one-way hashing with a random salt, so we cannot read or recover it.
  • Your school code.

Schedule information

  • The class names and room numbers you enter for your rotation.

Technical information

  • Your IP address is recorded when you create an account. It is used only to prevent abuse (for example, blocking accounts that violate the rules). It is not used for tracking or advertising.
  • A login token stored in your browser as a cookie so you stay signed in.

Optional information

  • If you submit a bug report, the text you write and your username are sent to your school's administrator.
  • Calendar (.ics) files you upload are stored only on your own device (in your browser), never on our servers.
  • If you enable the experimental ManageBac Sync (Beta) feature, exam and task data pulled from ManageBac is stored so it can be shown on your dashboard. This feature is opt-in and off by default.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email, phone number, or address.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third parties.
  • We do not track you across other websites.
  • We never store your ManageBac password.

How your information is used

  • To show you your personal schedule and keep it synced across your devices.
  • To let your school's administrator manage the schedule, holidays, and student accounts.
  • To keep the service working and prevent misuse.

Who can see your information

  • You — your own schedule and account.
  • Your school administrator — can see your username, whether you've set a schedule, bug reports you submit, and can reset or remove your account.
  • The service operator (super administrator) — has technical access needed to run and maintain the service across all schools.

Where your data is stored

Data is stored using Cloudflare's Workers KV storage. The app itself is hosted on GitHub Pages. These providers have their own security and privacy practices that by signing up, you agree to.

Your choices and rights

  • You can edit or clear your schedule at any time in the app.
  • You can log out to remove the login cookie from your device.
  • You can ask your school administrator to delete your account and its data.
  • You can leave the ManageBac Sync (Beta) feature turned off, or turn it off at any time.

Data retention

Your account and schedule are kept until you or an administrator delete them. Login tokens automatically expire after 30 days.

Security

Passwords are hashed and salted. Communication with the server uses HTTPS. However, no online service can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure. Please use a password you don't use anywhere important, and don't share your login.

Beta features

Some features are experimental (marked "Beta") and may not work reliably. They are optional and require your explicit consent to enable. They may change or be removed at any time.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app develops. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach out to your school administrator, use the "Report a bug" button in the app, or contact JJY Corp. Legal at JJYCorpLegal@consultant.com.
